Data Structures for Data Analysis for Data Visualization

Meelad Doroodchi

I wanted to analyze the most recent World Cup(2018) with the 2022 World cup looming this winter, but I wanted to compare the total # of goals scored in the 2018 World Cup to the total scored in 2014 to see the difference and what possibly could cause that difference. Each path during a World Cup run is different and I wanted to analyze the difference in 2014 and 2018!! In analyzing these two datasets, I looked through each dataset and looked at the variables named "Team, Goals For, and Goals Against. Using one data visualization/graph to compare these two datasets instead of putting them up side by side.
